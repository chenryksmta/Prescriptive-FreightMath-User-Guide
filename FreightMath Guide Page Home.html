About the Platform

Built on Activity-Based Costing — delivered through Power BI

FreightMath distributes every dollar of GL cost to the individual load using actual activity drivers — miles, hours under power, and load-specific charges. FM OR is the composite output: Core OR (the load) + IB OR (positioning) + OB OR (reload environment). All analysis runs on V_CLIENT_DATASET_ORDERS with ANALYZED_FLAG = 1.
