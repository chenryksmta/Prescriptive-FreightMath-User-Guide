Dedicated Pricing — Introduction

FreightMath includes a powerful Dedicated Pricing Calculator — a purpose-built tool for modeling the economics of Dedicated Contract Carriage. Whether you're quoting a new dedicated program or reviewing the profitability of an existing one, this tool gives you a structured weekly and monthly P&L proforma that is grounded in your actual cost structure.

The Dedicated Pricing Calculator handles per-load, per-mile, per-hour, and per-week rate structures — with configurable driver pay models, fleet sizing, fuel, overhead allocation, and custom upcharges. It generates client-facing proposals and carrier-use-only P&L proformas automatically.

For carriers actively pricing complex Dedicated Contract Carriage (DCC) opportunities, FreightMath offers an Expert Dedicated Pricing engagement — a hands-on advisory service where KSMTA works directly with your team to model route economics, structure a competitive rate proposal, and stress-test assumptions across volume and cost scenarios. This service is ideal for competitive bids or multi-route programs where precision pricing is a differentiator.
